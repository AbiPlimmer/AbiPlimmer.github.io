My current research focuses on how mantle dynamics are coupled with the lithosphere throughout the supercontinent cycle using 3D global mantle circulation models. To approach this problem I split a mantle circulation cycle into constituent large-scale processes; downwelling dynamics in the mantle and the effect of plate motion on slab sinking in the deep mantle, the coupling between deep mantle structures and plate tectonics, and the way in which lithosphere structure can affect mantle dynamics from supercontinent formation through to break up.

I am keen to advance this research to better understand the degree to which planetary interiors and surfaces evolve co-dependently. Especially, I am keen to understand how upwellings and downwellings both drive and are driven by plate tectonics and mantle circulation, and the role of deep mantle structures in facilitating these relationships.
